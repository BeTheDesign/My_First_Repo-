UX Skills

USER RESEARCH

For user research, my goal is to gain both the qualitative and quantitative results from user interviews in order to gain a solid understanding of human behaviors to identify goals and pain points into a user persona.

Tools:

Excel, Figma, Miro, and Otter

USER EMPATHY

For user empathy, my goal from user interviews is to categorize my findings into an empathy map in order to identify with the users and represent them when making decisions that may or may not serve the user best.

Tools:

Figma, Miro, and Google

WIREFRAMING & PROTOTYPING

For wireframing, my goal is to show a low fidelity layout content and functionality on a page which takes into account user needs and user journeys. For prototyping, I capture low-high fidelity design concepts for test on users.

Tools:

Figma, Adobe XD, and Invision

UX WRITING

For UX writing, my goal is to take the user on a design journey and guide them through a product successfully. I also want to improve the usability of an interface by writing text that is easy to follow and navigate.

Tools:

Figma, Adobe XD, and Invision
